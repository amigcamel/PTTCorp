What is PTT?

PTT (批踢踢) is a terminal-based bulletin board system (BBS) based in Taiwan, which contains over 20,000 discussion boards with more than 1.5 million registered users, and over 10,000 articles are posted everyday.

What is PTT Corpus

PTT Corpus is the first interactive interface which exploits PTT data for users to query words, make concordance, compare numbers of positive and negative comments of posts, etc.
